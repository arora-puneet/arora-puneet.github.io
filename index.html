Biography

I am an Assistant Professor of Economics at Amrut Mody School of Manegement, Ahmedabad University. My research interests are in the area of behavioral and experimental economics, applied in the contexts of education and finance. I have conducted field and lab experiments to study the individuals’ behavioral biases in educational and financial contexts. My research also studies the issues of identity based discrimination in different contexts.

I received my PhD in Economics degree in 2020 from Georgia State University, an MA degree in Economics degree in 2013 from Delhi School of Economics, and a BA Honors in Economics degree in 2009 from Hansraj College, University of Delhi.
